Have questions before getting started? Here are the most common questions about coaching, consulting, scheduling, and what to expect.

### 1) What is the difference between coaching and consulting?

Coaching helps you build clarity, mindset, communication, and consistent follow-through through a structured growth process.

Consulting provides expert analysis, strategy, and implementation support for specific personal or organizational goals and problems.

### 2) Do you provide therapy or clinical mental health treatment?

No. True Connections provides coaching and consulting services only. Services do not include therapy, diagnosis, or clinical treatment.

### 3) Who do you work with?

True Connections works with individuals, couples, leaders, professionals, teams, and small businesses.

### 4) What issues can coaching help with?

Common areas include life direction, confidence, mindset, emotional wellness, stress, resilience, relationships, communication, career growth, leadership growth, and work-life balance.

### 5) What issues can consulting help with?

Consulting supports strategy, management challenges, organizational development, change management, process improvement, workplace communication, leadership development, conflict resolution, and burnout resilience planning.
